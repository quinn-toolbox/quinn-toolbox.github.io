Que Anh (Quinn) Pham

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Massachusetts, Boston in the Developmental and Brain Sciences program. I earned my B.A. in Cognitive Science at Tufts University and my M.A. in Psychology at Boston University.

At UMass Boston, I am advised by Dr. Tashauna Blankenship. We are studying the development of episodic future thinking and Theory of Mind in children ages 2-5 years. My interests in developmental science are planning, decision making, and Theory of Mind. Broadly, I am curious about computational/ network neuroscience, machine learning, and philosophy of mind.

I am part of UMass Boston's Baby Lab. Check out their website for news and updated studies.
